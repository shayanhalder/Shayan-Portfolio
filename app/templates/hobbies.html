Hobbies

Piano

I've been playing piano since I was in 2nd grade. For me, it is a great way to pass time without technology or social media, and it helps me destress from academics in a healthy way. My favorite classical era is the Romantic era because of the amount of depth and expression that many songs in that era had. I've recently been looking into learning movie music on the piano from movies. My favorite movie music comes from Interstellar (2014) which many consider to be a masterpiece due to its soundtrack.
